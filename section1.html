Bowling at the HUB is one of my favorite affordable student activities to do on campus. It is a great activity to do by yourself or with a group of people. Shoe rental is included in the price per game which is a great plus!


Price

Monday - Thursday: $3.75 (Cosmic $4.00) per game

Friday - Saturday: $4.00 per game

Cosmic bowling is Mondays - Thursdays from 6:30 - 10:00pm. It includes music and party strobe lights during bowling


Hours

Monday - Thursday: 10:00am - 10:00pm

Friday - Saturday: 10:00am - 11:00pm


Special Deals

- On the 1st Monday of every month your first hour of bowling is free.

- On Tuesdays each game of bowling is $2.00
